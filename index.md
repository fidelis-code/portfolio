## 🛠️ Key Technical Projects (Case Studies)

### 1. Automated Multi-Layered Spatial Zoning
**Stack:** `Python (Pandas, GeoPandas)`, `QGIS`, `PostgreSQL`  
Designed and executed an automated spatial analysis pipeline to process **20+ thematic layers** for regional planning. 
*   **The Problem:** Manual zoning analysis was prone to human error and took weeks to update.
*   **The Solution:** Developed a Python script to automate intersection, clipping, and attribute joining across disparate datasets (topography, land cover, administrative boundaries).
*   **Impact:** Reduced processing time by 80% and provided a scalable model for **Land Use Land Cover (LULC)** change detection, essential for carbon sequestration modeling.

### 2. Concession & Boundary Verification Analysis
**Role:** Lead GIS Technician (Abt Associates / MCA Indonesia)  
**Context:** Multi-stakeholder Participatory Mapping Project.  
*   **The Challenge:** Overlapping land permits and "paper-only" boundaries create significant risks for carbon project integrity.
*   **The Action:** Standardized large-scale spatial datasets across district levels, conducting rigorous overlap analysis of concession permits. I led on-the-job training for local officials to maintain data veracity.
*   **The Result:** Established a "Single Map" framework that prevented legal conflicts and ensured spatial compliance—a direct precursor to the **additionality verification** required in carbon markets today.

**Stack:** `Looker Studio`, `Power BI`, `KoboToolbox`, `SQL`
